Our Story

Why We Started Visit Damascus

I grew up in Syria, walking through the lanes of the Old City and watching the sun set over Damascus from the heights of Mount Qasioun. I knew what every Syrian knows — that Damascus is extraordinary. But almost no one outside the Arab world fully understood how extraordinary.

In 2021, I launched Visit Damascus with one goal: to bring the world to the ancient capital. Not as a mass-tourism product, but as a carefully curated, deeply personal experience — guided by people who actually live here, love it, and know every hidden corner of this 8,000-year-old city.

We don't just show you the monuments. We tell you why they matter, who built them, and what it means that people still live and worship here today.

— Aleen Alhussain, Founder

Every tour is small-group (max 12), every guide is a local expert, and every meal is from a Damascus family kitchen — because the real city isn't in a museum. It's at the kitchen table.

What We Stand For

The principles behind every Visit Damascus experience

Authentic

No tourist traps. Every experience is designed by people who live here and love it.

Community First

90% of every booking goes directly to local guides, restaurants, and artisans.

Sustainable

Small groups, low impact, high respect for the sites and the people who live beside them.

Educational

We believe understanding history changes how you see the present. Every tour is a lesson in humanity.
